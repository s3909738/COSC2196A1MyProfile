I have a “Turbulent Advocate” personality, which means I am rather introverted, imaginative, enjoy helping others, organised and can be a bit of a perfectionist. I achieve my goals through constant improvement. I strive to do what’s right but will often prefer to keep the peace rather than challenge others. My learning style strongly leans towards Visual learning, finding it easier to make connections with visual cues and reading. I am a creative “Adventurer” which means I am passionate, easily inspired and expressive.
Although I may be an introvert, I can communicate within a team. I am more objective oriented with my communication, happy to discuss tasks and help my peers. I tend to avoid distractive conversations when focussed on a task, which can make people feel that I am socially disinterested. However, I am happy to chat and share banter when I am not focussed on work. I can be empathetic and understanding, willing to help my team members who may be going through issues. I also enjoy helping others out when I can contribute my knowledge.
I am capable of getting along with almost anyone. When looking for people to join a team with, I would hope to find people with good organisation skills, are open minded and unopinionated. People who are happy to discuss and explore issues from different angles. Being somewhat of a perfectionist, I would value being in a team with people who are willing to take in constructive feedback. I would also appreciate having group members who are willing to provide their feedback and ideas to help me improve on my work.
